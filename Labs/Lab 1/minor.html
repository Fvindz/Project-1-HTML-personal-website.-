ITEC Minor:

The purpose of the IT minor is to provide non-IT majors the opportunity to gain more computational skills and knowledge in IT.

Required courses

  • ITEC 2140: Programming Fundamentals
  • ITEC 2201: Intro To Info Systems
  • ITEC 3100: Intro To Networks
  • ITEC 3200: Intro To Databases
  • ITEC 3900: Professional Practice and Ethics
