Information Technology (ITEC)

Information Technology: ITEC is the multidisciplinary study, design, development, implementation, support, or management of computer-based systems. At GGC, we offer a bachelors degree in information technology.

Concentrations

Information technology offers 5 different concentrations:

Digital Media

This concentration is best suited for people seeking digital media jobs.

Enterprise Systems

This concentration is best suited for people seeking info tech jobs in business.

Software Development

This concentration is best suited for development jobs.

Systems and Security

This concentration is best suited for people looking to manage tech security.

Data Science and Analytics

This concentration is best suited for jobs pertaining to data.
